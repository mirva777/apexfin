Yo'l xaritasi

Roadmap

1

Idea

Foydalanuvchi muammolarini aniqlash, bozor tahlili, keksalar va yangi foydalanuvchilar bilan intervyular.

Identifying user problems, market analysis, interviews with elderly and new users.

2

Prototype

Ovozdan matnga konversiya + niyat aniqlash, faqat bir nechta asosiy to'lov stsenariylari uchun. Figma/klikabel prototiplar va kichik fokus-guruh bilan test.

Voice-to-text conversion + intent detection, for only a few key payment scenarios. Figma/clickable prototypes and testing with small focus groups.

3

MVP

Agrobank va asosiy to'lov provayderlari bilan integratsiya. Mobil va veb interfeys, keksalar uchun yirik elementlar, oddiy onboarding.

Integration with Agrobank and major payment providers. Mobile and web interface, large elements for elderly, simple onboarding.

4

Launched

Pilot loyihani Agrobank ekotizimida ishga tushirish. Foydalanuvchi fikr-mulohazalari asosida iteratsiya, yangi xizmat turlari qo'shish, boshqa bank va xizmatlarga kengaytirish.

Launching the pilot project in the Agrobank ecosystem. Iteration based on user feedback, adding new service types, expanding to other banks and services.

Nega bu loyiha muhim?

Why Is This Project Important?

Raqamli to'lovlar tez rivojlanmoqda, lekin ko'plab odamlar (ayniqsa keksalar) hali ham oflayn navbatlarga majbur. Ular onlayn interfeyslarda adashib qolishadi, xato qilishdan qo'rqishadi va natijada vaqt va energiya yo'qotishadi.

Digital payments are developing rapidly, but many people (especially the elderly) are still forced to stand in offline queues. They get lost in online interfaces, are afraid of making mistakes, and as a result waste time and energy.

Ovozli AI yordamchi raqamli moliyani hamma uchun ochiq va tushunarli qiladi. Texnologiya orqali to'lovlar oson, xavfsiz va ishonchli bo'ladi. Bu nafaqat foydalanuvchilarni qutqaradi, balki butun raqamli ekotizimni yanada samarali qiladi.

Voice AI assistant makes digital finance accessible and understandable for everyone. Through technology, payments become easy, secure and reliable. This not only saves users, but also makes the entire digital ecosystem more efficient.

Agrobank kabi yetakchi banklar bilan hamkorlik bu yechimni tezroq va ishonchli qilib, butun ekotizimga foyda keltiradi. Agrobank O'zbekistonda raqamli moliya va innovatsiyalarni rivojlantirishda eng oldinda turgan bankdir va bu loyiha uning missiyasiga to'liq mos keladi.

Partnership with leading banks like Agrobank makes this solution faster and more reliable, benefiting the entire ecosystem. Agrobank is at the forefront of developing digital finance and innovation in Uzbekistan, and this project fully aligns with its mission.
